About FASL_Official

Our History

Founded in 2018, Football Agency Sierra Leone emerged from a passion to showcase the incredible football talent in our nation. We started with just five players and have grown to become the leading football agency in Sierra Leone, representing over 50 talented athletes.

Our journey has been marked by numerous success stories, including players securing contracts with clubs in Europe, Asia, and across Africa.

Our Vision

To be the premier bridge connecting Sierra Leonean football talent with global opportunities, elevating the profile of our nation's football on the international stage.

We envision a future where Sierra Leone is recognized as a key source of world-class football talent, with our players competing at the highest levels worldwide.
